About Us.

MR Leos Welcome to the Leo Club of Moratuwa Rathmalana.

The power to turn a vision into reality is what makes a person a leader.

The Moratuwa-Rathmalana Leo Club is one of the greatest in Leo District 306 A1. From the beginning, Sri Lanka has been associated with Leo Multiple District 306, Sri Lanka. The Leo Club of Moratuwa-Rathmalana was founded on June 12, 1970, with the Lions Club of Moratuwa-Rathamalana in District 306 A1 providing sponsorship.

So far, we've accomplished a lot under the leadership and guidance of many people, and we're still committed to maintaining our high standards with 50+ years of excellence.
